Steps to protect your computer from cyberthreats

Follow these essential cybersecurity measures to keep your devices safe and secure.

Install a Firewall

Firewall controls the data that comes in and out of the computer system and block those that appears as threat.

Backup Everything

By copying your data in a separate location helps you restore your data by not having to give hackers what they demand from you.

Create a Boot Disk

allowing you to scan and clean your system before the operating system loads, preventing malware from taking hold and potentially causing significant damage

Install Security System

It blocks dangerous websites, stops hackers from accessing your files, and warns you if there’s a threat

Dont Open Unknown Emails

You should avoid opening unknown emails because they might contain harmful links or attachments.

Switch off your computer

Switching off your computer helps protect it from cyber threats because when it’s off, no one can access it through the internet or a network.
